Team phoenix

TEAM PHOENIX of Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology strives towards designing and fabricating a FORMULA styled car giving the world a Formula 1 thrill, ensuring automobile engineering enthusiast the pleasure and thrill of a formula one car while ensuring their safety.
Easy manufacturability, low cost and good aesthetics have been wonderfully woven into the Phoenix MotorSports Models. Analysis of the vehicles manufactured here are done with several iterations to obtain optimized inboard suspension system for comfortable handling and customized intake manifold is designed for superior performance. The vehicles are equipped with self developed data acquisition system for retrieving real time dynamic on road analysis data to improve vehicle performance.
